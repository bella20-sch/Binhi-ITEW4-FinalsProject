Overview

Stanley Ipkiss, a timid bank clerk, discovers a magical mask that gives him incredible powers, transforming him into a mischievous, confident, and charismatic character. His newfound persona leads him into dangerous situations, but also attracts the attention of Tina Carlyle, a nightclub singer. Things get complicated as Stanley begins to take on a crime lord and his henchmen.
